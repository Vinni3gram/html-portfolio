The Best Movies According to Vincent

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Pulp Fiction

This is my favorite movie by Quentin Tarantino because i'm a huge fan of his work and due to the amazing screenwriting, tale, pacing and it's mind-blowing dialogue.

Inglorious Basterds

This is another masterpiece from Quentin. I generally loved the idea of the "alternate history" and the acting was top-notch.

Shawshank Redemption

I love this movie for it's story, theme and also because I can listen to Morgan Freeman speak all day.
